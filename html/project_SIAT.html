Branding & Web Design

This project focuses on designing a unique business brand and developing a website that aligns with the brand's vibe. It is a collaborative effort between a two-person team and is scheduled for completion by the summer of 2023.

About the Brand

Our brand is named Aethel, the positioning of this brand is a luxury jewelry company with a minimalist design approach. One of the core characteristics of the brand is the passion for and pursuit of abstract art, integrating the subtle beauty of the natural world into our designs to create jewelry pieces that are dynamic and profound. Furthermore, to accentuate the brand's distinct aesthetic, we have chosen to showcase our unique perspective through the selection of collaborating designers. Each design product of the brand encapsulates profound significance, representing the pursuit of inner peace, harmony, and beauty, inspiring individuals to seek moments of tranquillity amidst the modern hustle and bustle of life. Once the main tone of the website was determined, we proceeded to design the interactive elements of the web pages to ensure a consistent and appealing appearance across various devices.

About the Design

Our brand's website style has been meticulously crafted to align perfectly with the brand's vibe and characteristics. The webpage exhibits a modern, minimalist, and exquisite design. The neutral gray background enhances content clarity while conveying the brand's gravitas and contemporary feel. The scrolling glass spheres further accentuate the brand's clarity and dynamism.

In terms of colour selection, we have employed soft, cool gray-blue and delicate gray tones. These hues not only enhance the web page's clarity but also resonate with the brand's nobility and tranquillity. Additionally, the visual elements on the webpage, such as images, icons, and typography, harmonize with the brand's modern artistic style.

The design elements of geometric shapes and streamlined logos amplify the webpage's modernity while unifying with the brand's pursuit of elegance and sophistication. Font choices have also been meticulously curated, utilizing modern and legible typography that further underscores the brand's contemporary and stylish attributes.

All in all, our brand's webpage style expertly combines colours, design elements, and layout to effectively encapsulate the core characteristics and values of the brand. The webpage not only provides visitors with a delightful visual experience but also aligns with the brand's ethos, showcasing a modern, luxurious, and high-quality brand image.

About the Development

The specific development of the website involved using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for layout and animation effects. As for generating images for the website, Photoshop and Figma were used as the primary tools.

Three Iterations & Reflection

The website underwent three significant iterations, from its initial concept to the final product. We boldly poured our ideas into creating the website's style guide and a few basic pages during the first iteration. During the second iteration, we added a popup feedback feature after users submitted forms and took inspiration from numerous luxury and jewelry brand websites to maintain a clean and tidy homepage. We decided to use a classical painting as the main image for the homepage and designed a simple layout.
